ini apa?

it's a random idea given form, i guess. a mix between geocaching and a digital art project. i really like exploring this island and finding all the little secrets in it and i think it's just really cool to stumble upon something unexpected.

this is my way of capturing that feeling, that sense of wonderment and fascination when you find something random and new for the first time.

how does it work?

whenever i make a new digicache, i'll post a pic of it on instagram as well as directions to find it.

if you end up finding it, DM me a pic of yourself with the qr-code on instagram, and i'll add you to the list of people who've found it.
